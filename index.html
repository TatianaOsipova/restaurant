Our History

Like any other original place, we have our own, special history. The founder of our restaurant was born and raised in Georgia and very often went to the mountains with his grandfather, who kept a flock of sheep and was a shepherd. In the mountains, on an open fire, they cooked traditional Georgian dishes. The food was very tasty and healthy. And he really wanted to introduce people to the dishes of Georgia!
